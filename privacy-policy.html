Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. About NANA COCO

NANA COCO ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application designed to help caregivers monitor and support elderly individuals. The app is operated by NANA COCO and is available on the Apple App Store.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, photo
  • Contact data: Phone number used for authentication
  • Health data: Medical conditions, diagnoses, medications, allergies, blood type, treating doctor information. This data is entered voluntarily by the caregiver.
  • Location data: Precise GPS location of the care recipient, collected periodically when the app is active
  • Device data: Device identifier for authentication and session management
  • Usage data: App interactions for improving the service

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide caregiver monitoring and SOS card functionality
  • To authenticate users via phone number OTP
  • To display the care recipient's location to their authorized caregiver
  • To send emergency alerts and notifications
  • To process subscription payments via Apple In-App Purchases

4. Health Data

NANA COCO is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Health information stored in the app is used solely to display emergency information to caregivers and first responders. We do not share health data with third parties except as required to operate the service.

5. Location Data

Location data is collected from the care recipient's device and shared only with their authorized caregiver. Location data is stored securely and is not sold or shared with third parties.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). Access to data is controlled through Row Level Security policies ensuring each user can only access their own data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of all your data at any time through the "Delete Account" feature within the app, which permanently removes all associated data within 72 hours.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, use the in-app account deletion feature or contact us at: privacy@nanococo.app

9. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication infrastructure
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing for subscriptions

10. Children

NANA COCO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@nanococo.app
